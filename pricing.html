Common Questions

FAQ

What kind of brands do you work with? +

Indian D2C and fashion brands doing ₹0–50L/year in revenue. We're specialists in post-purchase CX for early-stage brands that are growing but haven't invested in retention yet.

How fast can you start? +

Usually within 24–48 hours of your first message. The CX Audit is delivered in 48 hours, and the full Setup Package goes live in 7 days.

Do I need to provide access to my store? +

For the audit, no — we go through your store as a real customer. For the Setup Package, we'll need access to your email tool, helpdesk, and WhatsApp Business to configure everything.

What if I only want the audit? +

That's perfectly fine. Many brands start with the audit and implement fixes themselves using our prioritised list. You can always come back for the Setup Package later.

Is there a contract for the retainer? +

No long-term contracts. The Monthly Retainer is month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with 15 days notice. We earn your business every month.
