Case Studies
01

Fitlicious

Challenge

Loyal base of fitness-conscious customers in Jaipur but sitting at a 3.6-star Google rating. Unhappy customers left reviews while happy ones said nothing. Zero post-order follow-up system.

What We Did

Built a complete review generation system: WhatsApp follow-up 2 hours after delivery, direct Google review link in every order confirmation, packaging insert with QR code, and response scripts for negative reviews.

02

Krishna Sweets

Challenge

A well-known local sweet shop with decades of reputation but a weak online presence. Google rating dipped to 3.8 due to unresolved complaints, no system to encourage satisfied customers to share their experience.

What We Did

Set up QR code at the billing counter, trained counter staff on verbal prompts, wrote personalised responses for every negative review, and created a WhatsApp broadcast strategy for festive season regulars.

03

Bhagwati Paan Bhandaar

Challenge

One of Bikaner's most popular paan counters — but invisible online. Under 15 Google reviews despite serving hundreds daily. No strategy to capture goodwill digitally.

What We Did

Optimised Google Business profile with updated photos, correct categories, full description. Placed review QR card at counter and created a simple staff script. Consistent reviews started flowing within weeks.

04

Kora

Challenge

Beautiful products and growing Instagram following — but completely silent post-purchase experience. After payment, customers heard nothing until delivery. Repeat purchase rate under 12%.

What We Did

Built a 3-email post-purchase sequence, designed packaging insert with QR review code, wrote 6 support scripts for common queries, set up WhatsApp quick replies. Entire setup live within 7 days.
